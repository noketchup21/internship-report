### Overall Evaluation

**1. Suitability of Assigned Tasks**  
The tasks assigned throughout the internship aligned well with my academic background and helped me apply theoretical knowledge to real projects as well as using newly learned knowledge like AWS to the project.

**2. Quality of Guidance and Mentorship**  
The mentorship provided was supportive and clear, offering helpful explanations and allowing me to solve problems independently before receiving assistance.

**3. Learning and Skill Development**  
The internship offered valuable opportunities to learn new technologies, improve technical skills, and gain practical experience beyond what I had learned in school.

**4. Team Collaboration**  
The team demonstrated strong cooperation and communication, creating a supportive environment where members were willing to help each other when needed.

**5. Workplace Environment**  
The working environment was comfortable and positive, which helped me focus and be productive throughout the internship. However, the restroom facilities were insufficient to accommodate a large number of people.

**6. Company Culture**  
The company culture encouraged respect, teamwork, and positivity, making it easy to integrate even as a student intern.


**7. Internship Policies and Benefits**  
The internship offered a flexible work schedule. However, accessing the office required prior registration, and no additional allowances, such as for parking, were provided.

**8. Opportunities for Professional Growth**  
This internship contributed significantly to my long-term career development by offering real-world insights, industry practices, and exposure to professional workflows.

**9. Overall Satisfaction**  
Overall, the internship was a valuable experience that strengthened my skills, improved my confidence, and helped define my goals as a future software engineer.
